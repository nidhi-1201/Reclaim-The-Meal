About Us

An web-application that can help the environment go greener by saving the food from getting dump and
realsing the harmful methane gas.

As per the report of the United Nations, every year about 17% of cooked food is wasted which accounts for 931 million metric tons of food. Study Researchers are claiming that more than half of the produced food in Canada is being wasted. Walmart Foundation has reported that about 58 percent of Canadian food produced is thrown away at the end of the day because of not consumed. Many organizations are trying to reduce food production to resolve this issue, but that is not a solution to cut down on food wastage. To answer this subject, we developed an web-app that helps the environemnt from harmful emissions by saving leftovers from getting dump and aslso feeds the needy people through claiming that food. By connecting to our application, users can directly view the details of available food in the nearby restaurant and can visit if the food suits their requirements.
